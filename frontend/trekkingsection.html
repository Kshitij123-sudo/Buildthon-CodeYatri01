Kalsubai Peak

The 'Everest of Maharashtra', Kalsubai stands at 1646m, offering panoramic views of the Sahyadris. It is one of the most popular treks for nature and adventure enthusiasts alike.

A historic hill fort in Ahmednagar known for the Konkan Kada cliff and ancient caves. It offers challenging routes and breathtaking views of the Western Ghats.

The Rajgad Fort
