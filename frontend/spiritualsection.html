Explore the divine heritage of Maharashtra.

Trimbakeshwar

Dedicated to Lord Shiva, this is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas. The unique feature is the three-faced Linga embodying Tridev: Lord Brahma, Lord Vishnu, and Lord Shiva.

Grishneshwar

Steeped in spiritual significance, Grishneshwar holds a special place as the 12th and final Jyotirlinga of India, drawing devotees seeking blessings and solace.

Bhimashankar

Bhimashankar is a revered pilgrimage site and a nature lover's haven in the lush Sahyadri hills. It houses one of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Shirdi (Sai Baba Mandir)

Home to the spiritual guru Sai Baba. Thousands travel here daily to pay homage at the Samadhi Mandir and seek blessings from the saint known for his teachings on 'One God'.

Shegaon (Gajanan Maharaj)

Shri Gajanan Maharaj, a saint known for his miracles, appeared here in the late 19th century. His divine presence continues to attract devotees seeking peace and enlightenment.
