Hill Stations & Monsoon Trips

Escape to the cool and misty hills of Maharashtra.

Matheran

Asia's only automobile-free hill station, Matheran is a peaceful retreat in the Western Ghats. Famous for its red soil, dense forests, and breathtaking viewpoints like Panorama Point.

Know More

Lonavala

A popular getaway for Mumbai and Pune residents, Lonavala is known for its lush greenery, waterfalls, and lakes. It is especially beautiful during the monsoon season.

Know More

Khandala

Located near Lonavala, Khandala sits at 1,800 ft and offers scenic valleys and historic forts. Key attractions include Duke's Nose, Lion's Point, and Bhushi Lake.

Know More

Panchgani

Surrounded by five hills, Panchgani offers stunning views and a pleasant climate. Don't miss the Table Land, Parsi Point, and the famous strawberry farms.

Know More

Mahabaleshwar

The former summer capital of the Bombay Presidency, Mahabaleshwar is famous for its berries, majestic peaks, and ancient temples. A perfect destination for nature lovers.
