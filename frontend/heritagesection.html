Heritage & Historical Tour

Walk through the pages of history in Maharashtra.

The Ajanta Caves

A UNESCO World Heritage site, Ajanta Caves are a collection of 30 rock-cut Buddhist monuments renowned for their exquisite murals and sculptures dating back to the 2nd century BCE.

Know More

The Ellora Caves

Also known as Verul Leni, these 34 rock-cut caves feature Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain art. The magnificent Kailasa Temple, carved from a single rock, is a marvel of ancient engineering.

Know More

The Gateway of India

An iconic arch monument in Mumbai built to commemorate the royal visit in 1911. It stands as a symbol of the city and offers a beautiful view of the Arabian Sea.

Know More

Raigad Fort

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's capital, Raigad is a majestic hill fort in the Sahyadris. It is one of the strongest fortresses on the Deccan Plateau, rich in Maratha history.

Know More

Devagiri (Daulatabad) Fort

A historic hill fortress near Aurangabad known for its formidable defenses. It was once the capital of the Yadava dynasty and later the Sultanate of Delhi.
