Beach & Coastal Getaways

Discover the serene Konkan coastline.

Vengurla Beach

Vengurla Beach is a tranquil, pristine beach located in the Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra, near the Arabian Sea. It is known for its white sand, lush greenery, and calm waters, offering a peaceful escape for tourists.

Kihim Beach

Kihim Beach, a popular weekend getaway for those in Mumbai, is a small village located north of Alibag. Accessible by road and water, it is known for its unspoiled natural beauty and activities like horse cart rides and water sports.

Tarkarli Beach

Tarkarli Beach is known for its serene coastline, clear waters, and opportunities for water sports like snorkeling and scuba diving, attracting tourists seeking a blend of sea and Konkan culture.

Know More

Shiroda Beach

Shiroda Beach is a tranquil, unspoiled beach located in the Sindhudurg district. Known for its white sand, calm waters, and proximity to the Terekhol and Mochemad rivers, plus several famous temples nearby.

Know More

Alibaug Beach

Alibaug Beach is a popular destination located approximately 95 kilometers from Mumbai. It is the headquarters of the Raigad district and is famous for its serene beauty and sandy shores.
